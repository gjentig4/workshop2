# How To: Unlinking your integrated agenda from Teamleader Focus

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691059187345)

 

If you no longer wish to use an established link because, for example, you have switched from iCloud to Google Apps, then you can always disconnect this link.

- Navigate to your **icon user **in the top right-hand corner and click on “**Integrations/Marketplace**”

- Search for the application that you want to deactivate

- Click on the application and click on “**Manage**”

- Click on the **toggle** to turn off the application
