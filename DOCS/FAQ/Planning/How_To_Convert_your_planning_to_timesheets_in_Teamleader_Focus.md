# How To: Convert your planning to timesheets in Teamleader Focus

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153)

 

In Teamleader Focus, it’s possible to quickly generate timesheets for an entire period based on your Planning, saving you time and hassle. Read this article to find out how this can further boost your productivity and efficiency.

 

# Prefill multiple time trackings based on your Planning

If you’ve filled out your planning for a certain period of time, you can convert it to an entire timesheet with [multiple time trackings.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693832046865-How-To-Weekly-time-tracking-in-Teamleader-Focus)

- Go to **Planning**

- Click on the **arrow** next to your own user or the user you want to convert timesheets of
[*Note that only admins and leaders of a team can track time for another user.*](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691666760337-FAQ-Can-I-track-time-for-another-Teamleader-Focus-user)

- Click on **Convert to timesheet
![](https://lh7-eu.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXegS6c89K9W1_AyehEQE5gG2Cg7cCSmkGsjPp6R2oWCc5VqP0hgYGKdKeiBnmxh08z9IibQOQWpchFN4JzxoylY1k1L59pdR8VMyTnPW94f4_xP3o53_LdWPWRGUoBMYjrF216ruriAcbL4lFg1FqQDiLJ3?key=zunp6mlnIr8eoKKIhL48ow)

**

- Select a date range to import tasks from planning into your timesheets

- Click **Continue**

- You’ll be redirected to the screen to add multiple time trackings. Edit or delete time trackings if needed, or add additional time trackings.

- Click on **Add x time trackings** and they’ll be added to your timesheet.

## Add multiple time trackings from planning

Know that from within your timesheets module, you can also [add multiple time trackings based on your planning.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693832046865-How-To-Weekly-time-tracking-in-Teamleader-Focus)

- Go to **Timesheets** > **Add multiple time trackings**

- Choose **From planning**

- Select a** date range** and import the tasks from your planning.
