# How To: Use contact salutations in Teamleader Focus

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697763419281)

 

The way you address your customers in your email defines the tone you want to set for your business. Teamleader Focus makes it easy for you: just define the desired contact salutations, use the correct hashtag in your email template, and you’re set! You can find everything there is to know about contact salutations in this article.

 

Contacts salutations are for example used at the beginning of emails** to address** the contact. You can personalize them in the settings. They can be very useful and help you** set the tone **of your business with your customers. 

Examples are: “Sir”, “Dear”, “Hi”…

 

## How

- Go to **Settings > CRM > Preferences > Manage contact salutations. **
Here you can add, remove or change the order of the contact salutations.

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697720311569)

 

- Now go to **Contacts **> Select a contact > click on the **pencil **next to “Contact information” >** **choose the contact salutation you want and save the changes:

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697734732689)
 

## Add contact salutations to your templates

You can also use the title in almost all of your document templates. Simply add the **shortcodes** to your document and the info will appear.[ You can find more info about how to customize your templates in Teamleader Focus here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692647207057-How-To-Customizing-your-templates-in-Teamleader-Focus).

Once you have added this shortcode to your document, the title you chose for a contact will be shown on it. 

 

***Note**: You can also use the variable #CONTACT_SALUTATION in your templates for quotation introduction text.*

 

Tip: if you don't want to refer to contact salutations but just want to display the short text 'Attn' on your template, then you can use the shortcode $TEXT_ATTN$. This gets translated to the language of your customer so you don't need to use different template variations.

 

## Add contact salutations to your emails

- Go to **Settings > Email > Template for regular mails**. The hashtag for the contacts salutations is **#SALUTATION**. Make sure to put the hashtag where you want the salutations to be displayed in the email and save the changes.

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697749986449)

- Now click on the email address of the contact you want to send an email to. Select the template containing the salutation hashtag. The hashtag will be replaced by the info you wanted, including the salutation:

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697734863633)
