# How To: The power of Insights in Teamleader Focus

![SupportCentre_Howto.png](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/27754178276241)

 

As our customer, you want Teamleader Focus to help you grow your business through data-driven decisions. Our Insights module provides you with the knowledge needed to make those strategic decisions with confidence. By providing a helicopter view of your company’s current performance and future opportunities, we enable you to quickly make informed decisions that propel your business forward. 

 

*With Teamleader Focus, you're not merely managing the present; you're strategically mapping out your **path to future success. *** 

# The tools to make smart & informed decisions 

With easy to understand reports on your entire workflow, you gain a **complete understanding** of your business' performance and can think of where you want to be in the future. It empowers you to make smarter decisions and **grow your business** confidently.  

 

# Insights at your fingertips 

Insights allows you to have **instant access** to your insights at any given time. One click away! No more time wasting on exporting data to Excel and crunching the numbers yourself. Numerous insight reports allow you to instantly **zoom in on key metrics.** With **hyperlinks** and **drill-downs**, you are able to dive into the context. 

 

# Centralised in one platform

You benefit from having all your data **centralised in one platform**, leading to easier reporting and insights generation across different aspects of the business. This reduces the need for manual data consolidation and provides a holistic view of your operations.

 

# More time to improve & solve problems

Insights offers you massive **time gain**: the less time and effort it takes collecting insights the more time you have to work on the improvements. **Spot problems early and fix them** before they get out of control.

 

# Focus on what matters to you

Our Insights Library will get you far, however that’s only a starting point. Our pre-made reports are super useful, but every business is unique. By **creating your own easy-to-understand reports** via filters, group by and other reporting capabilities, you focus on what’s important to you. This way you get the answers you need, and not what we decide for you. 

 

# Do better every day & plan for the future

Understand where you're doing well at the moment and where you can improve, so you can keep getting better in the **future**. See what's coming, set targets and plan ahead to make sure your business stays on track.

 

# Use your money wisely

Know where your **money** is going and make sure you're spending it where it counts.
