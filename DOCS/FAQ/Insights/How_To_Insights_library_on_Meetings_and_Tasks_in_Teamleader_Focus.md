# Meetings

Below you'll find a list of all insights reports related to your meetings.
 

## Weekly Meetings done report

Overview of meetings done vs to-do by your team members.

 

## Deals Meeting Report

Overview of meetings linked to open and won deals that are done vs still to-do.

 

## Customer Meeting Report

Overview of meetings linked to customers that are done vs still to-do.

 

## Time tracking on Meetings Report

Breakdown of the time tracked on meetings by team members.

 

## Meeting locations

See where this week’s meetings are located.

 

# Tasks

Below you'll find a list of all insights reports related to your tasks.

 

## Monthly tasks report

A monthly overview of tasks by their status.
 

## Tasks in a project

An overview of tasks by projects and project groups.
