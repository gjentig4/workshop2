# FAQ: Can I export the reports in Insights?

![nnhja8IN0TUJMTH7JHXn2SYqRXo2WMosww.png](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/32423391701649)
Teamleader Focus makes it very easy for you to export an Insights report to either Excel, CSV or PDF. Read all about it in this article.

 

*Instead of having to export your reports in insights, you can very easily share them directly with your colleagues. This functionality is part of the **Advanced insights Booster** you can add on top of your package. *[*Find everything there is to know on the Insights Booster here. *](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/34775080480913-FAQ-What-are-the-key-features-of-the-Advanced-insights-Booster)

 

# What

Lots of businesses rely on reports for decision-making. To help with this, users can export the reports in Insights in order to save a record of their reports, print reports, and do complex calculations more easily.  
 

Depending on your needs, you can either export the report to:

- Excel

- CSV

- 
PDF
 

# 
Who 

To be able to export reports, [users must have certain toggles enabled in their user settings:](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691605666193-FAQ-Why-do-I-not-have-the-same-features-in-my-Teamleader-Focus-account-as-my-colleague)
 

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/37494410202897)![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/37494382126225)

- Access to Insights via Settings > Users > name of the user

- 
Access to ‘Export’ rights via Settings > Users > name of the user 
 

# How

To export a report in Insights, follow these steps:

- Navigate to **Insights**

- **Choose a report** from the report library

- 
*! What you see is what you get:*
Know that the groups and filters you apply to a report, as well as the columns that are present in your table, will influence the data that will be shown in your export.

- 
Click on **Export to Excel **or if you want to **export to CSV or PDF**, click on the **arrow **next to the button and select the right option:

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/32449491053329)
 

You can also choose **Copy link **if, instead of exporting, you just want to share the URL of this report with another colleague.

- 
Your choice will be remembered and preselected as the default when exporting another report later.
 

- 
Your export will be generated and you’ll receive a notification in the bottom of your screen to tell you when the export is ready:

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/32423844027025)
 

- 
Click on **Download **to download your export.

*Note regarding the PDF export: *If you’ve added a lot of columns to your table, it might be possible that the columns of your table cannot fit on the PDF page. In this case, we add the remaining columns on a new page.
