# How To: Deleting a segment in Teamleader Focus

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691008524177)

- Navigate, for example, to “**Contacts**” or “**Companies**”

- Select the segment that you want to adjust in the dropdown menu

- Click on the** trash bin** next to the dropdown menu

- Confirm by clicking “**Delete**”

Note: deleting a segment doesn’t mean that your data is removed. Only the segment will disappear.
