# How To: Adjusting a segment in Teamleader Focus

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153)

- Navigate to, for example, “**Contacts**” or “**Companies**”

- Select the segment that you want to adjust in the drop down menu

- Click on the** pencil** next to the drop down menu

- Make your adjustments

- Click “**Save**”
