# FAQ: What is the difference between contacts and companies?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691146599569)

 

There’s a difference between individual contacts and companies, as you can have different contacts in the same company. We recommend to use a 'contact' for individual people and use a 'company' for businesses.
