# How To: Sending invoices via Teamleader Focus

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153) 

Sending invoices with Teamleader Focus is simple and flexible. We offer four ways to send invoices: digitally via Peppol, by email, by post, or by downloading a PDF for manual sending. This article will guide you through each option to help streamline your invoicing process.

# Send via Peppol network (BE and LUX)

If you are a Belgian or Luxembourg customer, you can send your invoices via Peppol. Sending invoices digitally via the Peppol network in Teamleader Focus ensures fast, secure, and standardised transactions. Peppol allows businesses to send and receive electronic invoices directly between accounting systems, eliminating manual entry and reducing errors. [Find out all about it here.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/sections/29994922334353-Peppol)
 

# Send via email and InvoiceCloud

Sending invoices via email in Teamleader Focus is a convenient way to reach clients directly. By using InvoiceCloud, you can provide a secure link to a platform for clients to view and pay their invoices online. 

- [Read more about how to set whom the invoices are emailed to](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692222317201-How-To-How-can-I-set-whom-the-invoices-are-emailed-to)

- 
[Learn more about InvoiceCloud](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692202362257-How-To-Set-up-InvoiceCloud)
 

# Send via postal service

For Belgian and Dutch customers, Teamleader Focus offers the option to send invoices via traditional post. This service allows you to mail physical copies of your invoices, making it convenient for businesses that prefer paper invoices or need to accommodate clients who require them. [Discover more here.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691544686225-How-To-How-can-I-send-my-invoices-by-post)
 

# Download invoice PDF and send manually

A fourth option is to download the invoice PDF and just send it manually to your customer.
