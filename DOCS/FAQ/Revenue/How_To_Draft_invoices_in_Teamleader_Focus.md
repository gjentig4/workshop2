# How To: Draft invoices in Teamleader Focus

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153)

In this article we are going to explain to you how to make a draft invoice in Teamleader Focus.

 

1. Go to **Revenue** > **Invoices** and then click on **Add invoice **in the upper right corner.
![The header of the Invoices module in Teamleader Focus with the 'Add Invoice' button highlighted.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697871036689)

 

2. Fill in the information of your invoice and then click on **Save as draft**:![The invoice items editor in Teamleader Focus showing line items, VAT settings, and draft options.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25697893047953)

The draft invoice is now created and you can book it when you want it by clicking on** Book**. To know more about invoices in Teamleader Focus click [here.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692559900433-Getting-started-What-should-your-invoice-look-like)

 

*Notes:*

- *The draft invoice won't have a number. The invoice will receive a number once it has been booked.*

- *The draft invoice will not be synced with[ your bookkeeping tool](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692666955153-How-To-Exporting-invoices-from-Teamleader-Focus-to-import-into-your-bookkeeping-application). The invoice can be synced once it has been booked.*

- *To send this draft invoice to your customer before actually booking it and sending it via Teamleader Focus, you can click on **Download draft (pdf) **on the detail page of your invoice, and send this file manually.*
