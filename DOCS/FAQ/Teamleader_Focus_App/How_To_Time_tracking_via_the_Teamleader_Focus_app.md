# How To: Time tracking via the Teamleader Focus app

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153)

There are some interesting possibilities when it comes to tracking time via our Teamleader Focus app. You can add a time tracking manually or via the start/stop timer, or track time on an existing task. Read this article to learn more about these possibilities.

** **

## Add a time tracking manually

- Log in to the app

- Click on **Timer > + > Time tracking **or click on the **+ **on the home screen > **Time tracking**
**![The 'Add time tracking' form in the Teamleader Focus mobile app for logging work duration.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692789164561)**

- Choose to log time (duration) or to log specific hours

- Fill out a customer
*Note: *After you've linked a customer, a toggle 'Billable' will appear in which you can choose if the time tracking should be billable or not.

- Optionally, link it to an existing task

- Choose a work type

- Fill out a description

- Click on **Save**

## Add a time tracking via the start/stop timer 

- Log in to the app

- Click on **Timer**

- Click on the **+**

- Click on **Start timer **to start tracking time

- You can do other things in the meantime, the timer will keep going

- Click on the **Stop button** to end the time tracking: the tracking will be added to your list

- Clicking on your time tracking afterwards allows you to link it to a customer or a project, and choose a work type
** **

## Add a time tracking on an existing task** **

- Log in to the app

- Navigate to an existing task

- Click on the **+ **in the top right-hand corner

- Click on **T****ime tracking**

- Fill out the details of the time tracking

- Click on **Save**
