# FAQ: What can the Teamleader Focus app do?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691087072657)

 

The essentials of Teamleader Focus' award-winning tool inside an app for [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=eu.teamleader.focus) or [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/teamleader-focus/id6453793095)? It exists! Just fill in the same login details as for your web version and off you go!

 

Our app offers you a wide range of features:

- Easily manage your contacts on the road

A mobile [business card scanner](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691542613265-FAQ-How-do-I-scan-a-business-card-in-Teamleader-Focus) which allows you to upload the details from any business card straight to your Teamleader Focus CRM

- Add contacts to a company and define their function immediately

- View the activity feed/tracking of your contact or company

- Upload files on contact, company or deal level

- Plan tasks via the app

Mark tasks as done

- Track time on tasks

- Link tasks to deals and projects

- Reschedule tasks

- Check unscheduled tasks

- Reopen tasks

- Plan calls via the app

Dial calls via the app and log them as time tracking

- Mark calls as done and conveniently add a follow-up action or time tracking

- See when a call is due or whether it's done already

- Plan meetings via the app
List and download meeting reports

- 
[Easily track time via the app](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692785912721-How-To-Time-tracking-via-the-Teamleader-Focus-app)

Track time on projects
*Note: *It's currently not possible to access or add projects via the mobile app.

- Track time on existing tasks via the time tracker

- See linked deals of your contact/company in the blink of an eye

- Follow up on deals from anywhere

Add deals

- Change your deal phase

- Add files or remarks to your deal

- Follow up on quotations from anywhere

Add quotations

- Access and download your quotations (.pdf)

- Review and send your quotations

- Mark your quotations as accepted

- 
[Follow up on invoices from anywhere](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692769253009-How-To-Add-and-manage-invoices-on-the-Teamleader-Focus-app)

Add invoices

- Mark your existing invoices as booked

- Review and send your invoices
[Send your invoices via Peppol](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/30085139646225-How-To-Sending-e-invoices-via-Peppol-in-Teamleader-Focus)

- Change the status (paid/unpaid) of your invoices in a few clicks

- Access and download your invoices (.pdf)

- 
[Follow up on expenses from anywhere](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/37807697381265-How-To-Add-and-manage-expenses-via-the-mobile-app)

Add expenses

- Review your expenses

- Have customers sign mobile [work orders](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690927285777-How-To-From-work-order-to-invoice) on the spot

Access both your upcoming and your past work orders

- Assign several employees to one work order

- Add work hours across several days

- The app is also optimised for iPad / Android tablet use
