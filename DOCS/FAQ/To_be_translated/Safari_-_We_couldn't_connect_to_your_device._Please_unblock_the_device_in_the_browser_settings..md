# Safari - We couldn't connect to your device. Please unblock the device in the browser settings.

You haven't given Vectera permission to access your device yet. But it's simple! Just follow these steps:

- 
Open the Safari preferences at the top of your screen (or press ⌘ + ,) 

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25694766378257)

- Open the tab 'Websites' (1) and choose Camera and Microphone (2). Select 'Ask' or 'Allow' (3) for Vectera![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25694829435665)
