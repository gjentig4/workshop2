# FAQ: How can I share my microphone and camera in Firefox (Android)?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25694812099601)

To allow the Customer Meeting add-on to access your microphone and camera using Firefox in Android, you will have to give permissions in your device Settings:

- Navigate to Settings on your Android device's homepage

- Scroll to Apps

- Search for FireFox

- Select 'Permissions'

- Make sure that Camera and Microphone are activated
