# Google Chrome - We couldn't connect to your device. Please unblock the device in the browser settings.

You haven't given Vectera permission to access your device yet. But it's simple! Just follow these steps:

1. Click on the camera icon on the right side of the address bar.

2. Select ‘Always allow [https://www.vectera.com](https://www.vectera.com) to access your camera and microphone’.

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25694765495185)

3. Click on 'Done'.
4. Click on 'CAM OFF' and/or 'MIC OFF' to start using your camera and microphone

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25694778664977)

5. The buttons will now turn green, to show you are using your camera and microphone
