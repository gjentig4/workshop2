# FAQ: What does an orange star in the agenda stand for?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692102031889)

Â 

If there is an orange star in the day view of your agenda, it means that there is something planned for the whole day in the personal agenda. This is only visible if your [calendar is synchronized with your personal agenda](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690957593745-How-To-Integrate-calendars-with-Teamleader-Focus).
