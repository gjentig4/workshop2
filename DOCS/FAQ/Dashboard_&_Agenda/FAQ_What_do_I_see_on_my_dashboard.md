# FAQ: What do I see on my dashboard?

![A graphic banner featuring the text 'FAQ' with a magnifying glass and a document icon.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690748323089)

The dashboard is the first thing you see when logging into Teamleader Focus. Here you find out what values you actually get presented at this moment.

## Tasks

- the 6 most recent overdue tasks of the last 7 days

- all tasks planned for today

- the 6 tasks that are next up in the next 7 days which are not yet scheduled in your agenda.Â 

- tasks that are due today and which you finish today are crossed out and stay like that for the rest of the day.

## Meetings

- the 6 most recent overdue meetings of the last 7 days

- all meetings planned for today

- the 6 meetings that are next up in the next 7 days

- meetings that are due today and which you finish today are crossed out and stay like that for the rest of the day.

## Calls

- the 6 most recent overdue calls of the last 7 days

- all calls planned for today

- the 6 calls that are next up in the next 7 days

- calls that are due today and which you finish today are crossed out and stay like that for the rest of the day.
