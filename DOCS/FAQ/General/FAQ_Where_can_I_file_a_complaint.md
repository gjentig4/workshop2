# FAQ: Where can I file a complaint?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692699174289)

 

We are very sorry to hear that you are unsatisfied with our service. Our constant aim is to serve our customers in the best way possible and we continuously strive to improve.

 

Therefore, we are very happy to listen to your feedback. Please [contact our support team](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/requests/new?ticket_form_id=25911134514065), so that we can see what we can do for you and that we can keep on improving.
