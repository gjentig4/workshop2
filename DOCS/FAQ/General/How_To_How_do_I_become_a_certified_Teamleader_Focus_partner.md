# How To: How do I become a certified Teamleader Focus partner?

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153)

 

Teamleader offers companies or individuals who wish to sell Teamleader Focus the opportunity to **engage in a long-term collaborative partnership**. To become a Teamleader Focus partner, however, you will need to meet certain criteria and complete a number of steps.

 

**Note: The Teamleader Focus Partner Program differs from the Referral Program. To learn more about the difference between Referrals and Partners, read ‘**[**What is the difference between referrals and partners?**](https://support.focus.prd.teamleader.eu/hc/en-us/articles/25691127029393)**’. **

 

## How do I apply?

- Visit [https://www.teamleader.eu/partner](https://www.teamleader.eu/partner).

- Leave your credentials at the bottom of this page.

- Our Partner Manager will contact you to assess whether there is **a mutual fit**.

- If so, you will be contracted as an official Teamleader Focus partner.

- Together, we will develop a personal plan for **sales** and **product training**, along with an **action plan**.

- You will receive** a commission percentage** of the gross profit on a sale.

 

If you wonder whether you could become a Teamleader Focus Partner, be sure to read ‘[What is expected of me as a Teamleader Focus Partner?](https://support.focus.prd.teamleader.eu/hc/en-us/articles/25691139857681)’.
