# How To: Adding pictures to quotations

![A 'How To' tutorial banner featuring a wrench and gear illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25690662955153)

 

There are different ways to add a picture to your quotation, depending on what you are searching for.

- Adding a picture of an article

- Adding a fixed picture to all your quotations

- Add pictures to the introduction text of your quotation

## Adding a picture of an article

- "Products" and click on the product you would like to add a picture to

- Click on the** icon** next to the name of the product

- Upload your picture in JPEG format

- This picture will automatically be shown on your quotations when choosing this article. Note that the shortcode $LONG_DESCRIPTION$ needs to be present on your template to display the image.

## Adding a fixed picture to all your quotations

- Go to **Settings **> **Document ****layout **> **Quotations**

- Click on **Download current template** (in case you do this the first time, you need to choose **Download example template**)

- Add the picture in the word document

- Save the word document

- Go back to Teamleader Focus and click on **Upload template**

- Choose the document you just added your picture to.

- Click **Open**

- The picture will be visible on all newly created quotations, the existing ones will not be affected automatically.

## Add pictures to the introduction text of your quotation

- Navigate to your quotation and edit it, or [create a new quotation](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693612763409-FAQ-How-can-I-create-a-quotation-in-Teamleader-Focus).

- In the 'Introduction text', click on the **image icon**

- Click on **Upload **to choose a file > Click **Send it to the Server**

- Adjust the settings of the picture if you want to

- Click “**OK**”

- The picture is now added to your quotation text for this particular quotation.*Remark**: You can also add pictures to your template(s) for quotation layout. That means you don't need to insert the picture every time but you can just select the template.*

- Add product(s) to your quotation.

- Click **Save and preview **or **Save.**
