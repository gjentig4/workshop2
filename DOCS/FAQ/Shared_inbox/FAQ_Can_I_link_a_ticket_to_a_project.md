# FAQ: Can I link a ticket to a project?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25693634076177)

When creating or receiving tickets you might want to add them to one of your projects to keep track of all communication with your customer. You can! Read how to link your project to your ticket here.

## Linking your project

To link a project to a ticket:

- 
Go to **Tickets**

- 
Click on the ticket which you want to link to a project

- 
Click on the **pencil **next to the ticket number to edit the fields

- 
In the field project, **choose **one of your projects and one of your phases

*Note*: The first phase of the project will automatically be selected but you can of course choose another phase in the field ‘Phase’.

 

In your project, you can find an overview of the linked tickets:

- 
Navigate to the **project**

- 
Click on the tab **Project info** in the header

- 
Scroll down to ‘Open tickets’![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25693634151057)

## Time tracking project via ticket
