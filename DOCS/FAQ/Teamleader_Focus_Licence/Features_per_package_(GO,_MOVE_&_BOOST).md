# Insights Booster

You can also add a Booster on top of your package: the** Insights Booster.**
 

Alongside the [existing features of the Insights module included in every account](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/28006164415889-How-To-Insights-in-Teamleader-Focus) and the [advantages the module brings](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/27754060283409-How-To-The-power-of-Insights-in-Teamleader-Focus), you’ll be able to enrich your Insights even further thanks to our Insights Booster.
 

With the Booster you gain advanced reporting capabilities that boost your efficiency and create alignment on KPIs. The Insights Booster offers you these four key functionalities: 

- Share your reports internally amongst colleagues

- Visualise your data with conditional formatting

- Customise your metrics by adding formulas to your reports

- Compare your data across time and track performance

 

[Read all about the Booster and adding it to your package here.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/34775080480913-FAQ-What-are-the-key-features-of-the-Advanced-insights-Booster)
