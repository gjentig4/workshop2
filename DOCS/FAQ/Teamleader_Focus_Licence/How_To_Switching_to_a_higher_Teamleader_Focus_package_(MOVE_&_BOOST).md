# How To: Switching to a higher Teamleader Focus package (MOVE & BOOST)

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25693396893969)

 

*! This article is only relevant if you have one of our older pricing plans GO, MOVE or BOOST. If you have one of our newer plans (SMART, GROW, FLOW), you can't switch to MOVE/BOOST. *[*Click this link if you want to know how to switch to a higher GROW/FLOW package.*](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25696138907537-How-To-Switching-to-a-higher-Teamleader-Focus-package-GROW-FLOW)

 

You are currently on a GO or MOVE plan and you’d like to switch to a higher Teamleader Focus package? Read this article to find out how to do this.

 

## How to switch

 

Only [Teamleader administrators](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691974365841-FAQ-What-are-the-rights-of-an-admin) can upgrade the pricing plan of the account. Follow these steps if you have admin rights and want to upgrade your plan:

 

- Click on your **user ****icon **in the top right-hand corner in Teamleader

- Go to **Manage licence**

- Click on **Change package**

- Choose the package you want to switch to

You’ll have immediate access to all features of this package

- [Check the features per package here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25697618837009-Features-per-package-GO-MOVE-BOOST)

- In the next step you'll see your prorated price. Upon renewal of your subscription you receive an invoice with the complete package price.

 

## When to switch

You can switch to a higher package **anytime you want to**. The switch will be **immediate **and you don’t have to wait for renewal of your subscription. You’ll receive an invoice with a prorated price charge.

 

If you want to know how to add new users to your Teamleader Focus licence, you can read [this article on how to buy new users.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692210395793-How-To-How-do-I-buy-a-new-user-licence-for-my-Teamleader-Focus-account)
