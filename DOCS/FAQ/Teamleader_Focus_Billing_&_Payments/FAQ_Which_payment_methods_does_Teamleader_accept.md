# FAQ: Which payment methods does Teamleader accept?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25693410199697)

 

Whether you want to pay your invoice quarterly or yearly, switch to a higher package or add an extra user to your Teamleader Focus licence, you will need to insert a means of payment.

 

To make the payment process as seamless as possible, we accept the following payment methods:

- 
**Credit card (Master, Visa, American Express)**
The amount will be charged automatically via your credit card.

- 
**SEPA direct debit**
The amount will be charged automatically via direct debit.

 

Do note that **bank transfer is not a payment option**.

 

In case you want to [switch to another payment method](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692577467665-FAQ-How-can-I-adapt-my-Teamleader-Focus-subscription), click the link to find out how.
