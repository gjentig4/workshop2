# Getting started: What should your invoice look like?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692570158993)

 

Teamleader Focus offers you a basic example template, which can be modified and updated under Settings > Document layout. This way, you'll have the [opportunity to use your own house style](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692647207057-How-To-Customizing-your-templates-in-Teamleader-Focus).

 

If you need multiple types of invoices, be sure to read [this article](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691627848977-FAQ-How-can-I-add-different-layout-variations-of-my-documents-e-g-quotations-or-invoices). Equally interesting may be the fact that you can add your very own [general terms and conditions](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690882757137-How-To-Upload-general-terms-and-conditions).

 

**Note**: The templates for credit notes match the ones for invoices. Click here to learn how you can [create a credit note](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692421867409-FAQ-How-do-I-create-a-credit-note).

 

That's it: you're all set to create your invoices! Click [here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/categories/24833596585105) for an overview of all articles on how to get started with Teamleader Focus.
