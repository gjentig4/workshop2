# Getting started: What is the idea behind time tracking in Teamleader Focus?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692453143057)

 

Tracking time actually serves two purposes. First of all, you can track time and then invoice it to your customer. Even if you don't want to invoice your tracked time, timesheets offer you the second option to get an overview of what you and your colleagues have been up to and how long they worked on things.
