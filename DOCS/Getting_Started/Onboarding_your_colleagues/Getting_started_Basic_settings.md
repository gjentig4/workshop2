# Getting started: Basic settings

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25693904619537)

Are you a new Teamleader Focus user or do you want to get a new employee started with the tool? Send this article to every new user to make sure they can get started as soon as possible.

## Personal settings

After logging in for the first time, you can set up [your personal settings](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691914966673-How-To-Setting-up-my-personal-Teamleader-Focus-account). Go to the user icon in the upper right-hand corner and click on your name. Here you can add a picture, select your language, create a signature, [activate MFA](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692015506577-How-To-Set-up-MFA-security) and choose if you want to receive [notifications via email](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691648678801-FAQ-Can-I-deactivate-the-email-notifications-in-my-Teamleader-Focus-account).

 

## Link your calendar

Never want to forget meetings anymore? Then link your [external calendar](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690957593745-How-To-Integrate-calendars-with-Teamleader-Focus) to Teamleader Focus!

 

## Link and track your emails

[Track your incoming and outgoing email](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692591287697-How-To-Tracking-emails-to-Teamleader-Focus) directly with the right contact or company by linking your email to Teamleader Focus.

 

## Mobile app

With our [mobile app](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691083613201-FAQ-What-can-the-Teamleader-Focus-app-do), you'll always have your customer details in your pocket, wherever you go. Manage contacts, register calls, schedule tasks and meetings.

 

## Lead capture Booster

Schedule, meet and engage with customers online, on top of all the other functionality our tool already brings. [Read all about what it is and how to activate it.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25694859794577-How-To-Activate-and-use-the-Lead-capture-Booster)

 

 

Be sure to read [our tips & tricks article on letting your team get started with Teamleader Focus.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25697706519697-Getting-started-How-can-I-let-my-team-get-started-with-Teamleader-Focus)
