# Getting started: What is the idea behind the calendar in Teamleader Focus?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692424904593)

 

In short: Being up to date! You find your agenda in two different places: In the dashboard, by clicking on the Teamleader Focus logo, and in the calendar module. Both parts are structured in different manners to serve various purposes.
