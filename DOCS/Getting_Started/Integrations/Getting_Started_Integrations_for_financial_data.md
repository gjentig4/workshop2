# Getting Started: Integrations for financial data

## 
![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25695286920337)What exactly is the Marketplace?

The [Teamleader Focus Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/be/en) is a central platform that allows you to browse through all available integrations with Teamleader Focus. A connected business is a smart business. Integrating your business systems enables a better customer experience and improves your internal workflow.

 

## How do integrations for financial data help?

Obtain financial information on the companies you are involved with. Are they financially stable? How did they do last year? How many employees do they have? And best of all: this information is immediately available to everyone in your organisation, thanks to these integrations:

 

- [Trends Top ](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/teamleader/trends-top/1fa089)

- [KlantAlert](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/pm-networking/klant-alert/0e24a3)

- 
[Companyweb](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/nitor-be-bv/companyweb/985da3)
