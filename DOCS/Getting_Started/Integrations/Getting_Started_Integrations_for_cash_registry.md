# Getting Started: Integrations for cash registry

## 
![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25695286566417)What exactly is the Marketplace?

The [Teamleader Focus Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/be/en) is a central platform that allows you to browse through all available integrations with Teamleader Focus. A connected business is a smart business. Integrating your business systems enables a better customer experience and improves your internal workflow.

You are looking for a POS (Point Of Sale) system that connects with Teamleader? This ensures you win time, makes your work more efficient and lets you enjoy from the best of both software solutions. Choose a solution out of these cash register integrations:

 

- [CloudPos](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/cloud-pos/cloud-pos/e51bb0)

- [Welpos Payments ](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/stefan-wellerdieck/welpos-payments/8ba376)
