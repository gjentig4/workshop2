# Getting Started: Integrations for exporting data in Teamleader Focus

## 
![A 'LET'S START' introductory banner with a paper plane illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691002060177)What exactly is the Marketplace?

The [Teamleader Focus Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/be/en) is a central platform that allows you to browse through all available integrations with Teamleader Focus. A connected business is a smart business. Integrating your business systems enables a better customer experience and improves your internal workflow.

 

## How do integrations for exports help?

Exporting data? It can be done with any of these integrations. Suppose you need to calculate commissions in Excel, or draw up extensive statistics on your business. You can easily export data from Teamleader Focus, and if need be, re-import again afterwards.

 

- [Google Sheets](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/cogetix/google-sheets/043023)

- [Invantive Composition in Word](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/invantive/invantive-composition/70da56)

- [Invantive Data Loader](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/invantive/invantive-data-loader/b83d3d)

- [Invantive Query Tool](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/invantive/invantive-query-tool/29e3a3)

- 
[Adverity](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/adverity-gmb-h/adverity/ee36ad) 

- [Prestashop (APIcenter)](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/ap-icenter/prestashop/63d5bf)

 

If you work in construction and want to create certificates or attestations or site reports based on your Teamleader Focus data, check out these links:

- [ArchiSnapper](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/archi-snapper/archi-snapper/ae2159)

- [Climapulse](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/be/en/detail/climapulse-nv/climapulse-service/fb4e27)

 

Looking for an export to a Dashboard solution? Look [here](https://support.focus.prd.teamleader.eu/hc/en-us/articles/25695225926033).
