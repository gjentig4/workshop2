# Getting Started: Integrations for time tracking

## 
![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25695218760721)What exactly is the Marketplace?

The [Teamleader Focus Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/be/en) is a central platform that allows you to browse through all available integrations with Teamleader Focus. A connected business is a smart business. Integrating your business systems enables a better customer experience and improves your internal workflow.

 

## How do integrations for time tracking help?

Time tracking is a crucial asset to project management. You can use our time tracking integrations to track the time you spend on certain tasks, or even import time tracking from other tools! These integrations help you save time and work smarter.  

 

- [GeoDynamics IntelliTracer](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/geo-dynamics/geo-dynamics-intelli-tracer/9d6e21)

- [OutSmart](https://marketplace.focus.teamleader.eu/eu/en/detail/werkbon-app/out-smart/fdf70d)
