# Getting started: Integrations for collection of unpaid invoices

What exactly is the Marketplace?

The [Teamleader Focus Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/be/en) is a central platform that allows you to browse through all available integrations with Teamleader Focus. A connected business is a smart business. Integrating your business systems enables a better customer experience and improves your internal workflow.

 

## How do integrations for collection of unpaid invoices help?

Connect your Teamleader Focus-account to online platforms that help in collecting your outstanding invoices. These integrations will save you a significant amount of time per month in financial accounting and reduction of bad debt.
