# Getting Started: Integrations for lead generation

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25695322035089)

## What exactly is the Marketplace?

The [Teamleader Focus Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/be/en) is a central platform that allows you to browse through all available integrations with Teamleader Focus. A connected business is a smart business. Integrating your business systems enables a better customer experience and improves your internal workflow.

 

## How do integrations for lead generation help?

By automating the process of capturing and sending leads to your Teamleader Focus CRM, you can save time and reduce the risk of errors. Additionally you can ensure that they receive timely and relevant follow-up. This can help increase your chances of converting leads into customers. Learn more in our blog ['How to attract customers'](https://www.teamleader.eu/blog/how-to-attract-customers).

 

But first: instead of having to use one of our integrations offered in our Marketplace, we have **our very own integrated contact forms for lead generation in Teamleader Focus**. Read all about it [here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25695775141649-How-To-Contact-forms-and-how-to-create-them). One of the advantages of using these contact forms over our integrations are Lead capturing in one central tool: manage everything in one place, and all leads from the contact form are stored within your Teamleader Focus account
