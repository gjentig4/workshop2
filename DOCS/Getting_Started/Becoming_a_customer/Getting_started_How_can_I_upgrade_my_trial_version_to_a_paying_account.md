# Getting started: How can I upgrade my trial version to a paying account?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691165465361)

Go to your trial account and click on the banner **Upgrade Now **in the top right-hand corner.

- You'll then see the following page with your recommended package: 
![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691194475537)

- Choose extra users if you need them. Each package will have 2 users by default already.

- Need extra features? Then [check and compare](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25696098299537-Features-per-package-SMART-GROW-FLOW) the functionalities of all packages.

- Enhance your package with Boosters, such as the Lead capture Booster, the work orders Booster or Project management.

- On this same page, you'll also need to choose your billing cycle via the toggle above: bill quarterly or bill yearly. A yearly renewal of your subscription will save you 17% on your total invoice!

- In the next step, you can choose if you need additional help with setting up your account or not. Read more about the types of assistance we offer [here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693558117905-Need-personal-assistance).

- 
Enter your billing details. Don't forget to fill in your VAT number if you are VAT liable. Then, choose a payment method. More info on payment methods can be found [here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693391339153-FAQ-Which-payment-methods-does-Teamleader-accept).

- 
You'll now get redirected to a new page where you can find an overview of your Teamleader Focus subscription. Find information on your next invoice in the top-right hand corner.You can always return to this page later by clicking on your **user ****icon** in the top right-hand corner in Teamleader Focus > **Manage licence**.

Once your account is activated, you will receive an email with information on your invoice, our support options and some useful tips and tricks to get started.
