# Need personal assistance?

We’ve got your back. In addition to our free resources you have the option to get personalised assistance from one of our onboarding managers. If you’d like to arrange one or more personalised sessions, let us know or go to [your license page](https://focus.teamleader.eu/web/settings/subscription/onboarding) by clicking on your image in the top right-hand corner of your account and choosing 'Manage License' 'Assistance'.

![Onboarding service options in Teamleader Focus including self-service and paid assistance packages.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25693575939345)

 

You have multiple options:

 

## A full Do It With Help trajectory

This includes:

- Up to 5 online sessions with our experts, on top of the analysis of your workflow and preparation

- Assistance with the set-up, tips, tricks and best practices via our online training platform

- Train colleagues in using Teamleader Focus within your company. 

- Includes the import of your existing data (3 modules) and creation of your document layouts

 

Price: One-time fee of **750 euro excl. VAT**

 

## External partner

You’d rather leave the implementation to a professional? One of our Service Partners can come **on-site** to implement Teamleader Focus in your company. 

 

Price: One-time fee of **1.250 euros excl. VAT**

 

## A One-hour session which can be used in one of two ways

- As a workflow session

 

An onboarding manager will assist you in optimising your account during a one-hour session. We will answer your questions and give you tips & tricks.

 

- For imports or the creation of your documents

 

Don't have time to take care of data imports or the creation of document templates for quotations, invoices, ...? An onboarding manager will create these documents for you according to your needs and upload them into Teamleader Focus.

 

Price: One-time fee of **175 euro excl. VAT.**
