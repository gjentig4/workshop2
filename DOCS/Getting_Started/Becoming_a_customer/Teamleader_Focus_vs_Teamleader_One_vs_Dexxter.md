# Teamleader Focus vs Teamleader One vs Dexxter

Regardless of whether you are an experienced entrepreneur, an expanding small to medium-sized business, or an emerging solopreneur, Teamleader is equipped to meet your needs. Explore our solutions here:

- 
[Teamleader One](https://www.one.teamleader.eu/en):  the go-to and affordable (e-)invoicing software that self-employed entrepreneurs can count on.

-
