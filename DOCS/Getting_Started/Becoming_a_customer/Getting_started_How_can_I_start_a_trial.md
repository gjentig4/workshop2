# Getting started: How can I start a trial?

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691160407697)

 

Discover for yourself how Teamleader Focus makes your administration faster, easier and more organised. Start a demo and:

- experience how easy invoicing, creating quotations and time tracking can be;

- discover all the features at your own pace;

- see if Teamleader Focus is the right fit for your business;

- test Teamleader Focus with real customers, projects or invoices.

 

You can do this completely free of charge for 14 days. No credit card, no hassle.

 

# How to start a demo

You can get started in just two steps:

- Go to: [https://signup.focus.teamleader.eu/](https://signup.focus.teamleader.eu/)

- Fill in the form and start your free demo period. 
 

It doesn't get any easier than that!

The demo period will **not** be automatically renewed.
