# Getting started: How can you get an overview of who is working when on what?

![A 'LET'S START' introductory banner with a paper plane illustration on a light blue background.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25691002060177)

 

- *What reporting is needed?*

- *In what way would you like to check up on your colleagues, also regarding holidays and targets? What are the processes now?*

 

Teamleader Focus offers[ holiday planning](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690696602897-How-To-Configuring-leave-in-Teamleader-Focus) so that all information is in one spot and easy to be retrieved. Furthermore, it is possible to export an[ HR report](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691716494225-How-To-Exporting-reports-from-Teamleader-Focus), even daily if desired, to remain up to date with what your workers are up to.

 

In addition it is possible to set a [week scheme](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691870457873-FAQ-How-can-I-set-a-week-schedule-in-Teamleader-Focus), a[ lunch break](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691604826257-FAQ-How-can-I-set-a-lunch-break) and[ closing dates](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691773689873-How-To-Working-with-closing-days-in-Teamleader-Focus) in order to paint a realistic picture of the tracked time. A final way of keeping track of your colleagues' work are[ targets](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691312824465-How-To-Adding-Targets).

 

Our Insights module also offers several reports on time tracking, such as the time efficiency per employee. [Be sure to check it out.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/28242396199953-How-To-Insights-library-on-CRM-Sales-and-Productivity-in-Teamleader-Focus)

 

You are now all set for HR. Check [here](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/categories/24833596585105) for all articles on how you can get started with Teamleader Focus.
