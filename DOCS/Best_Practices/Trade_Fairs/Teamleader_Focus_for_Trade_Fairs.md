# Teamleader Focus for Trade Fairs

![Illustrative banner with the text 'TEAMLEADER FOR' and icons representing CRM contact management.](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692850503441)

 

Is your company going to be at a trade fair? Don’t forget to use Teamleader Focus to get the most out of it. Keep reading to discover all our tips!

 

# Before the fair

[Create a segment](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690816108305-How-To-Creating-a-segment-in-Teamleader-Focus) of the customers or prospects you would like to talk to and inform them using our [email marketing tool integrations](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/eu/en/category/email-marketing). Do you prefer sending an invitation via post? [Export the addresses](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692402812945-FAQ-Do-I-have-to-add-the-company-address-to-contacts-in-Teamleader-Focus) or download labels using the ‘actions’ button. 

 

# During the fair

Make sure you have a laptop or tablet with a [landing page](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693169806481-How-To-Link-an-online-form-to-Teamleader-Focus-Web2Lead-integrations) that automatically creates a contact and [deal](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692361765777-Getting-started-What-is-a-deal) in Teamleader Focus, so colleagues that are staying in the office can [follow up](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692333511441-Getting-started-How-do-you-want-to-structure-and-follow-up-on-your-deals) immediately. If you use [Zapier](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25693119585425-How-To-Integrate-Teamleader-Focus-with-Zapier), you can even have the deal source filled in. This way you can easily create a list of the deals that resulted from this fair using [segments](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690816108305-How-To-Creating-a-segment-in-Teamleader-Focus). Zapier also allows you to add an [opt-in](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692799115281-FAQ-What-does-the-checkbox-Opt-in-marketing-mails-mean) to the landing page, so you can send mailings afterwards.

 

Did you only manage to get a business card? [Use our app to scan it](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691542613265-FAQ-How-do-I-scan-a-business-card-in-Teamleader-Focus) and immediately register your visitor’s contact information in Teamleader Focus. 

 

# After the fair

If you didn’t use a landing page during the fair, you can also [import the contact information to Teamleader Focus](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691800529297-How-To-Importing-contacts-and-companies-to-Teamleader-Focus) afterwards. Don’t forget to add a tag to the import mentioning the fair, so you know where you talked to this person. 

 

Use this tag to [create a segment](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690816108305-How-To-Creating-a-segment-in-Teamleader-Focus) of the visitors of your booth, so you can send out [mailings](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/eu/en/category/email-marketing). Do keep the [GDPR rules](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/sections/25690708572305-GDPR) in mind though!

 

Finally, use our [deal statistics](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/28242396199953-How-To-Insights-library-on-CRM-Sales-and-Productivity-in-Teamleader-Focus) to find out exactly what you got out of this fair.
