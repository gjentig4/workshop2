# Teamleader Focus for New Users

![](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/article_attachments/25692899189521)

 

New to Teamleader Focus and not really sure where to begin? This quick start guide will get you up and running in no time. If you want more detailed guidelines, check out our[ Getting Started articles](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/categories/24833596585105).

 

## 1. Your personal settings

Go to the icon or your picture in the top right-hand corner and click on 'My profile'. Here, you can add a picture, select your preferred language, create a signature and decide if you want to[ receive notification mails](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691648678801-FAQ-Can-I-deactivate-the-email-notifications-in-my-Teamleader-Focus-account) or [activate MFA](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692015506577-How-To-Set-up-MFA-security).

 

## 2. Completing your company information

Go to **Settings **> **Company ****information > click on the company entity > Edit **to complete your company info such as your address, payment information and uploading your logo. You can also upload your[ terms and conditions](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690882757137-How-To-Upload-general-terms-and-conditions). This is important when you start sending quotations, invoices or other documents, as your company details will be filled in automatically.

 

## 3. Adding colleagues

Go to **Settings **> **Users **to[ add accounts for your colleagues](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690695274513-How-To-Adding-new-users-to-your-Teamleader-Focus-account) and manage their access rights.

 

## 4. Importing your existing data

Think about how you want to structure your CRM using[ tags](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690854640529-How-To-Using-tags-in-Teamleader-Focus) and[ custom fields](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692628832017-How-To-Creating-a-custom-field-in-Teamleader-Focus), and[ import your existing contacts and companies](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691800529297-How-To-Importing-contacts-and-companies-to-Teamleader-Focus). It is also possible to import existing[ products](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692618041233-How-To-Importing-products-into-Teamleader-Focus),[ deals](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691668093329-How-To-Importing-deals-into-Teamleader-Focus),[ projects](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691671131025-How-To-Importing-projects-into-Teamleader-Focus),[ invoices](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691675511825-How-To-Importing-invoices-into-Teamleader-Focus) and[ time tracking](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25691706103569-How-To-Importing-time-tracking-into-Teamleader-Focus), but think about which information is really worth keeping first.

 

## 5. Sending mails from Teamleader Focus

- First make sure you can send mails using your own name by [setting up your custom domain](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692624924561-How-To-Setting-your-custom-domain-for-sending-emails-from-Teamleader-Focus).

- Create a[ general signature for all your mails](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690729809425-How-To-Setting-a-signature-for-outgoing-emails) under Settings > Email > Signatures for outgoing email and add templates under ‘templates for regular emails’.
 

## 6. Implementing your house style

[Adapt your templates](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692647207057-How-To-Customizing-your-templates-in-Teamleader-Focus) so you can send documents to your customers in your own style.

 

## 7. Integrating Teamleader Focus with your favourite tools

[Integrate your agenda](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25690957593745-How-To-Integrate-calendars-with-Teamleader-Focus) and[ track your mails](https://support.focus.teamleader.eu/hc/en-150/articles/25692591287697-How-To-Tracking-emails-to-Teamleader-Focus). Check out our[ Marketplace](https://marketplace.teamleader.eu/eu/en) to see all our possible integrations.
