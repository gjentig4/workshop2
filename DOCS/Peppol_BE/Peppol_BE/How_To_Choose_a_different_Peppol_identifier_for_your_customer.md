# Why change the Peppol identifier?

By default, Teamleader Focus sends Peppol documents to your customerâ€™s primary identifier (for Belgium this is based on the KBO number). There are several reasons why you may need to select a different Peppol identifier for a customer:

- Your customer has multiple registered Peppol identifiers.

- The default identifier is outdated or incorrect.

- The recipient has specific preferences for receiving documents under a different identifier.

- The document needs to be directed to a specific department or branch within a larger organisation.
