## Peppol ID

Quickly summarised, this is how it works:

- You need software to send and receive e-invoices via Peppol and this is where Teamleader Focus comes in.

- Your customer needs to be registered on Peppol too, no matter which tool (could be Teamleader Focus or not).

- The software gives you something to identify yourself with: aÂ **Peppol ID**. For Belgium, this is a unique code and consists of the prefix 0208 combined with your KBO number, or 9925 combined with your VAT number.

- You can send the UBL together with the PDF from Teamleader Focus to your customer's software, and it's readable. You send based on your Peppol ID to the other participant's Peppol ID.
